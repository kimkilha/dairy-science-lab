Advanced Research in Lactic Acid Bacteria, Fermentation, and Probiotic Development

About Us

The Dairy Science Lab at Hankyong National University is dedicated to cutting-edge research in dairy fermentation, lactic acid bacteria characterization, and the development of innovative probiotic food products. Our research contributes to both scientific advancement and practical applications in the food industry.
